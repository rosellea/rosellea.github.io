TOP 3 BEST SLEEPING SPOTS

  1. Near a Window: Sunbeam paradise! I can watch birds, squirrels, and everything that moves outside while staying cozy and warm inside. Bonus: the breeze ruffles my fur just right as I doze off mid-birdwatch.

  2. Human’s Bed: Ah, the ultimate cloud of comfort! Soft blankets, warm pillows, and it smells just like my hooman. Perfect for long, luxurious naps where I can stretch out like royalty. Best part? No one can resist giving me belly rubs when they see me here.

  3. In the Garden: Ah, the great outdoors—where the grass is cool and the breeze is just right. I can sprawl out under the sun, hidden among the flowers, with all the smells of nature around me. Birds chirping, leaves rustling—it's like a natural lullaby. Just watch out for the occasional bug landing on my tail... but hey, that’s part of the fun!
